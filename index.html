Hi! My name is Cristiand, I am 21 years old, and I have a passion for building and creating massive projects in Minecraft. I love pushing creativity to the next level and bringing unique visions to life.

I was chosen as Colombia official representative in the MrBeast Gaming global competition! One of my most ambitious Minecraft projects: recreating my country's most iconic locations—snowy mountains, jungles with jaguars, the walls of Cartagena, stunning cathedrals, coffee plantations, and modern cities—all with a lot of love. I had more fun than ever building it, and I feel like I managed to show the world how beautiful and diverse Colombia is. The result filled me with immense pride.
